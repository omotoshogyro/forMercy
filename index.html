ABOUT $UTDOGE

Untouchables Doge token is a decentralized community-driven token which derived its inspiration from the likes of the Untouchables 1987 film based on Al Capone and a group of elite lawmen lead by Jimmy Malone looking for justice during the prohibition. These unregulated digital currencies are acting as an outlet to young thriving creators and Untouchables Doge is excited to further that torch.

BINANCE SMART CHAIN

Untouchables Doge token is built entirely on the Binance Smart Chain which helps to eliminate cumbersome transaction fees and introduce a seamless interaction within the community.

COMMUNITY ORIENTED

What makes Untouchables Doge unique is its approach towards incentivization of community efforts and one-of-a-kind incorporation of distribution across all $UTDOGE holders. Having a marketing allocation (2%) on each transaction secures the future development of all things $UTDOGE. This plus a community orientated platform and environment ensures the healthy growth of any project.
